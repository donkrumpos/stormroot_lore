# Khel’Druun, the First Fracture  
### Breaker of Boundaries • Maker of Shards • Father of Unraveling

Khel’Druun is the patron of invention  
and the herald of collapse.

---

## Appearance  
A humanoid silhouette made of floating stone shards,  
fractured geometry, and iridescent void.

---

## Domains  
- Chaos, entropy, possibility  
- Art that destroys norms  
- Breaking and remaking  
- Temporal distortions

---

## Symbols  
- Broken circle  
- Floating shards  
- Rift-sigil  

**Colors:** vantablack, opalescent violet  
**Material:** Fracture Orb  

---

## Offerings  
- Broken tools  
- Impossible shapes  
- Shards of failed creations

---

## Shrine (Fraymarch)  
A plaza of singing fractures  
and floating stone platforms.

---

## Minor Spirits  
- **Glimmernix Shardfellow**  
- **Crinklefen Riftprig**  
- **Skiddlewretch Jumblewick**

---

## Corrupted Aspect  
### *Khelzra the Unmade*  
In **Shatterweft Chasm**, memory and matter splice apart.

---

## Mythic Wound  
Everything he creates eventually breaks—  
including himself.

---

## Role  
His champions are innovators,  
rule-breakers,  
and sometimes accidental heralds of change.
