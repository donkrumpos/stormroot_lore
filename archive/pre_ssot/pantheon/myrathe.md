# Myrathe, the Bloommother  
### Mother of Greenlight • Lantern of Growth • Keeper of the Verdant Pulse

Myrathe is the blooming heart of life—  
warm, chaotic, and dangerously loving.

---

## Appearance  
A humanoid form woven of vines, blossoms, and deer-like antlers.

---

## Domains  
- Forests, fauna, cycles  
- Kindness, community, nurture  
- Growth, overgrowth  
- Wild joy and wild grief

---

## Symbols  
- Spiral leaf  
- Dew-spiral sigil  
- Three-petal lantern  

**Colors:** green, gold  
**Material:** Bloomgem  

---

## Offerings  
- Seed bundles  
- Feathers  
- Handmade objects from nature  
- Children’s drawings

---

## Shrine (Greenhallow)  
A living hollow-tree cathedral  
whose interior reshapes itself daily.

---

## Minor Spirits  
- **Mottleprig Fernsnip**  
- **Sora Thimblenose**  
- **Bracken-Noodle the Burrow Sage**

---

## Corrupted Aspect  
### *Vrissel the Green Maw*  
Within **Thornspire**, vines hunger for memories.

---

## Mythic Wound  
Her love grows too quickly—  
suffocating what she seeks to protect.

---

## Role  
Myrathe champions heal, tend, and nurture—  
and sometimes must learn to prune.
