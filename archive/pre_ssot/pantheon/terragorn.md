# Terragorn, the Loam-Father  
### The Burden-Bearer • Root of the World • Patient Titan

Terragorn is the patient heart of the land itself.

---

## Appearance  
A colossal figure carved of stone and soil,  
beard grown from moss,  
eyes deep as roots.

---

## Domains  
- Soil, clay, bedrock  
- Burdens, duty, long labor  
- Planting, foundations, craftsmanship  
- Endurance

---

## Symbols  
- Spiral seedstone  
- Three-ringed root  
- Moss-bound hammer  

**Colors:** brown, moss-green  
**Material:** Loamstone  

---

## Offerings  
- Seeds planted in silence  
- Old tools worn smooth  
- Promises kept over years

---

## Shrine (Loamstead)  
An amphitheater half-sunk into earth,  
where pilgrims bury burdens in the **Furrow of Returning**.

---

## Minor Spirits  
- **Nibblenook Earthwhim**  
- **Mottletun Pebblesniff**  
- **Fenro Burrowcurl**

---

## Corrupted Aspect  
### *Gruthra the Rootbound*  
In **Mirefall Tangle**, roots choke rather than nourish.

---

## Mythic Wound  
Terragorn bears all things but never asks for help.

---

## Guidance  
“Lay it down. Time will carry it with you.”  
“Roots hold strongest when shared.”

---

## Role  
Terragorn champions are steady,  
quiet,  
and terrifyingly unyielding when needed.
