# Serith Noxbloom  
### The Blooming Truth • Lady of Spores • Whisper of the Violet Vein

Serith governs toxins, insight,  
dangerous truths, and transformative decay.

---

## Appearance  
A slender figure woven from vines, spores,  
and bioluminescent petals.  
Eyes glow ultraviolet.

---

## Domains  
- Poison, toxin, venom  
- Truth as painful revelation  
- Alchemy, spore magic  
- Growth through rot

---

## Symbols  
- Hollow-petal bloom  
- Venom droplet  
- Spore spiral  

**Colors:** sickly green → ultraviolet violet  
**Material:** Nox Gem  

---

## Offerings  
- Truth written in toxin-laced ink  
- Hollow gourds carved with sigils  
- Vials of rare venoms

---

## Shrine (Fenwick Hollow)  
A fungal-pillared temple glowing with bioluminescence,  
priests masked with carved gourds.

---

## Minor Spirits  
- **Mottleprig Spoorwink**  
- **Nibfen Gleamsap**  
- **Twizzlethorn Sweetsick**

---

## Corrupted Aspect  
### *Seruzahl, the Violet Plague*  
In **Noxspire**, truth becomes venom.

---

## Mythic Wound  
Serith cannot stop unveiling truths  
—even when they shatter those who hear them.

---

## Role  
Her champions see what others refuse to—  
but must learn the mercy of silence.
