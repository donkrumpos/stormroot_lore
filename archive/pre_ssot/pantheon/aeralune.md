# Aeralune Whisperwind  
### Feather-Caller • Sky’s Daughter • keeper of Fleeting Songs

Aeralune governs the wandering forces—  
wind, thought, movement, and change.

---

## Appearance  
A bright, airy being with flowing hair  
woven from clouds,  
footsteps leaving no imprint.

---

## Domains  
- Air, wind, skyways  
- Travel, messages  
- Inspiration, wandering  
- Spontaneity

---

## Symbols  
- Spiral feather  
- Wind-chime sigil  
- Sky-pearl  

**Colors:** sky-blue, pearl white  
**Material:** Aerolight Pearl  

---

## Offerings  
- Paper kites  
- Windchimes  
- Breath-songs  
- Traveling vows

---

## Shrine (Aerlum Ridge)  
A cliffside aviary with floating bridges  
woven of tensile silk.

---

## Minor Spirits  
- **Fenniflip Breezelet**  
- **Pipwhistle Castlenip**  
- **Thistlewren Driftwing**

---

## Corrupted Aspect  
### *Ulfen Sighwind*  
In **Galehollow Ruin**, wind strips away names.

---

## Mythic Wound  
Aeralune cannot stay in one place or one thought.

---

## Role  
Her champions are couriers of fate,  
wanderers,  
and bringers of unexpected grace.
