# Gravorn Forgeheart  
### Hammer of the Deep • Maker of Makers • Master of Stoneblood Metal

Gravorn is the anvil upon which civilization is shaped.

---

## Appearance  
A massive, broad-shouldered figure  
with skin of hammered iron  
and eyes like molten metal.

---

## Domains  
- Metal, ore, stone veins  
- Craftsmanship  
- Innovation, smithing  
- Perseverance

---

## Symbols  
- Split anvil  
- Metal-vein spiral  
- Forge spark  

**Colors:** iron-grey, molten gold  
**Material:** Ironheart Lith  

---

## Offerings  
- Finished crafts  
- Broken tools repaired  
- Hammerstones  
- Acts of honest labor

---

## Shrine (Ironwell)  
A forge built around a meteorite core,  
its anvil ringing differently for each pilgrim.

---

## Minor Spirits  
- **Brindlebit Orewhisk**  
- **Tockhammer Sprig**  
- **Myrn Coalzip**

---

## Corrupted Aspect  
### *Gorhask the Rustking*  
In **Rustcrown Cradle**, metal remembers envy.

---

## Mythic Wound  
Nothing Gravorn makes ever feels “good enough.”

---

## Role  
His champions are creators—  
but must avoid becoming hoarders.
