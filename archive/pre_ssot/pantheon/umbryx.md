# Umbryx, Night-Serpent of Reflection  
### The Mirrorcoil • Whisper in Silver Water • Keeper of Hidden Truths

Umbryx governs moonlight,  
reflection, dream, and longing.

---

## Appearance  
A long serpentine being woven from moonlight and dark water.  
Eyes like shifting mirrors.

---

## Domains  
- Moonlight  
- Dreams, introspection  
- Secrets, illusions  
- Tidal shadows

---

## Symbols  
- Crescent-serpent  
- Mirror shard  
- Silver droplet  

**Colors:** indigo, silver  
**Material:** Gloamcrystal  

---

## Offerings  
- Secrets whispered into still water  
- Mirror shards  
- Dream-ink sigils

---

## Shrine (Moondrift)  
A crescent pool that reflects only moonlight,  
even at noon.

---

## Minor Spirits  
- **Gloamwisk Shimmerweft**  
- **Nai’Mirren Sigh-Lantern**  
- **Wispadoon Tideshadow**

---

## Corrupted Aspect  
### *Zhelmir the Many-Faced*  
Within **Gloamsorrow**, reflections whisper lies.

---

## Mythic Wound  
Umbryx cannot tell truth from reflection.

---

## Role  
His champions are dream-walkers, illusionists,  
and seekers of hidden truths.
