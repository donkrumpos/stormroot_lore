# Eirundal, Lantern at the Crossing  
### Guardian of Last Doors • Moth-Stag King • Keeper of Soft Endings

Eirundal is gentle death—  
a guide, not a taker.

---

## Appearance  
A tall, soft-robed figure  
with a lantern of featherlight flame  
and horns like glowing moth antennae.

---

## Domains  
- Death, passage, endings  
- Memory release  
- Candlelight and riverstones  
- Dreams of closure

---

## Symbols  
- Feathered lantern  
- Moth-stag sigil  
- River-pebble circle  

**Colors:** grey, amber  
**Material:** Lantern Opal  

---

## Offerings  
- Last letters  
- Locks of hair  
- Silent vigil candles  
- Stones held for 40 days

---

## Shrine (Stillgrave)  
A stone arch over a shallow moonlit river—  
the **Crossing Pool**.

---

## Minor Spirits  
- **Wickerfen Glowmoth**  
- **Pipwallow Laststep**  
- **Thren-lu Whispercoat**

---

## Corrupted Aspect  
### *Eirgrym, the Stilled*  
In **The Bone Lantern**, death clings instead of releasing.

---

## Mythic Wound  
He cannot cross the river he guards.

---

## Role  
His champions bring mercy and closure—  
and fight despair with gentleness.
