# Sunspire  
### The Dawn-Watch City • Bastion of Radiance

Sunspire stands atop a mesa, its golden tower catching both dawn and dusk.

---

## Architecture & Aesthetic  
- Goldstone spires  
- Sunwrought glass facades  
- Radiant mosaics  
- Reflective canals

---

## Culture  
**Sunrise Oaths** — vows renewed each morning.  
**The Dazzle Parade** — mirrored costumes that reflect light.  
**Solstice Illuminations** — entire city glows through prism lanterns.

---

## Notable NPCs  
- **Halius Brightmantle**, prideful paladin  
- **Thyra Dawnweaver**, sun-glass artisan  
- **Pulse the Radiant Moth**, semi-divine familiar

---

## The Severing’s Wound  
Light sometimes refuses to cast shadows—  
or casts perfect, still ones that do not move.

---

## Adventure Seeds  
- A shadow refuses to obey its owner.  
- A dawn that never rises.  
- A sunbeam that leads somewhere impossible.

---

## Gateway to Blazebound  
A cracked sun-prism in the tower’s base.
