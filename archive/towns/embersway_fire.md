# Embersway  
### The Forge-Town of Bright Oaths • Hearth of Makers

Built around the volcanic **Dawnforge**, Embersway is a city of artisans, oath-keepers, and fierce debates held over open flame.

---

## Architecture & Aesthetic  
- Lava-fed forge canals  
- Obsidian walkways etched with runes  
- Every home has a hearth altar  
- Flame-kites drifting in updrafts  

---

## Culture  
**The Ember Oath** — promises burned into metal scraps and worn.  
**Sparkduels** — ritual arguments where flame reveals truth.  
**Cinderfast** — festival of rapid crafting competitions.

---

## Notable NPCs  
- **Tharo Emberwright**, smith whose work glows even when cooled  
- **Kessa Cindervale**, fire-dancer with prophetic burns  
- **Ironbelch**, a soot-elemental who adopted the town

---

## The Severing’s Wound  
Fires sometimes flare with emotions, not fuel.  
Arguments spontaneously ignite literal sparks.

---

## Adventure Seeds  
- A forge-flame whispers someone’s hidden fear.  
- The Dawnforge belches a molten egg.  
- Ember Oaths begin rewriting themselves.

---

## Gateway to Ashen Vault  
Hidden beneath the great forge:  
a stair of cooled obsidian leading into red-lit depths.
