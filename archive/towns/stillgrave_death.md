# Stillgrave  
### The Quiet Crossing • Lantern of Last Steps

Stillgrave is a serene valley town, built around a moonlit river that gently carries offerings for the dead.

---

## Architecture & Aesthetic  
- Stone lanterns lining riverbanks  
- Bonecarved stairs  
- Feather-moth banners  
- Pale stone houses with soft acoustics

---

## Culture  
**The Night of Soft Endings** — a ceremony to honor unfinished stories.  
**Pebble-Carrying** — stones carried for 40 days before release.  
**Dream Wakes** — interpretive gatherings to resolve grief.

---

## Notable NPCs  
- **Elder Vennul**, who remembers everyone’s last words  
- **Threnna Mothkiss**, priestess who raises mourning moths  
- **Kelso Driftstep**, ferryman who never looks back

---

## The Severing’s Wound  
The river sometimes refuses to carry offerings away—  
returning them upstream.

---

## Adventure Seeds  
- Spirits refuse passage.  
- A lantern sinks and glows from underwater.  
- A ghost asks to be forgotten.

---

## Gateway to The Bone Lantern  
A stone arch in the riverbed, visible only at full moon.
