## Overview

Modrik’s Causeway binds Rothigport to the island of Noctisbard — stone tethering driftwood.

## Mythic Tone

- Ore insists on permanence.
- Water resists being owned.

## Narrative Function

This site embodies the **identity fracture** of Rothigport:
Is it a drifting haven, or a rooted port?

## System Impact

- alters trade routes
- weakens certain tidal rituals
- strengthens land-based authority

## Canonical Truths

- The Causeway changed Rothigport forever.
- Not all change is welcomed.
- Some rites fail within sight of stone.
