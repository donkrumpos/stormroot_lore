# Stormrest  
### The Cliffside Roaring-Town • Aetherborne Haven

Stormrest clings to cliffs battered by perpetual winds.  
Lightning rods double as streetlights.

---

## Architecture & Aesthetic  
- Rope bridges swaying over abyss  
- Lightning towers  
- Shingle roofs carved with wind-runes  
- Sky-sails rigged to houses

---

## Culture  
**Storm-Leaps** — ritual cliff-jumps with wind harnesses.  
**Boltfeast** — feast held only during a lightning storm.  
**The Breath Test** — meditation beneath gale winds.

---

## Notable NPCs  
- **Aero “Bolt” Finn**, thrill-seeker courier  
- **Thalra Windmantle**, sky-knight  
- **Zipspark**, storm-sprite mascot

---

## The Severing’s Wound  
Storms act on emotion—  
anxious days bring furious winds.

## Adventure Seeds  
- A storm forms a face.  
- A lightning rod begins speaking.  
- Aerial beasts circle the cliffs, waiting.
