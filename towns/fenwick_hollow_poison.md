# Fenwick Hollow  
### Glowfen Village • Hamlet of the Whisper Bloom

Fenwick Hollow glows at night with bioluminescent spores drifting through the streets.

---

## Architecture & Aesthetic  
- Hollowed gourds as houses  
- Fungal pillars  
- Purple-lit pathways  
- Alchemical windmills

---

## Culture  
**Truth-Telling Nights** — stories told while drinking mild toxin brews.  
**The Violet Trial** — subtle test of perception.  
**Gourd Masks** — used to read emotions.

---

## Notable NPCs  
- **Bloss Nittlewick**, spore-priestess  
- **Tarn Spindlecut**, potion thief  
- **Wibblespoar**, gossiping mushroom sprite

---

## The Severing’s Wound  
Spores whisper truths with too much sharpness.

---

## Adventure Seeds  
- A spore cloud reveals everyone’s secrets at once.  
- A masked festival where no one can remove their mask.  
- A bloom that speaks in three voices.

---

## Gateway to Noxspire  
A glowing violet pit beneath the largest gourdhouse.
