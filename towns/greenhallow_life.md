# Greenhallow  
### The Living Village • Heart of the Bloomwilds

Greenhallow is built inside and among giant living trees,  
linked by rope-bridges and singing vines.

---

## Architecture & Aesthetic  
- Hollow-tree homes with shifting interiors  
- Platforms grown from living wood  
- Bioluminescent vine lanterns  
- Moss carpets

---

## Culture  
**Heartshare Feasts** — the entire town cooks for travelers.  
**Bloom Naming** — children receive new names during major growth spurts.  
**The Kindroot Pledge** — binding agreements witnessed by vines.

---

## Notable NPCs  
- **Auntie Bloss**, matriarch whose hair blooms seasonally  
- **Ferran Greenthorn**, gentle druid with a talking fern  
- **Pip Thimbleleaf**, prankster vine-skipper

---

## The Severing’s Wound  
Vines sometimes attach themselves emotionally—  
literally clinging to people who feel lonely.

---

## Adventure Seeds  
- A tree grows a door no one remembers.  
- A child’s imaginary friend becomes real.  
- Gifts begin giving their own gifts.

---

## Gateway to Thornspire  
A massive hollow trunk whose bark bleeds amber sap.
