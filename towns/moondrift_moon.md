# Moondrift  
### The Silver-Tide Hamlet • Village of Mirror Pools

Moondrift floats partly on water, partly on stilts,  
and partly on *mirrors that behave like water.*

---

## Architecture & Aesthetic  
- Silverwood stilts  
- Mirror pools reflecting only moonlight  
- Indigo-shingled roofs  
- Hanging lanterns shaped like droplets

---

## Culture  
**Reflection Vigil** — write a thought, burn it, watch the smoke reflect.  
**Dream-Mending** — communal dream interpretations.  
**Tidelight Walks** — wanderings along moonlit sandbars.

---

## Notable NPCs  
- **Seren Vel**, dreamsmith who repairs nightmares  
- **Glimmer**, a reflection who became real  
- **Brask Tideglass**, mirror artisan

---

## The Severing’s Wound  
Reflections sometimes detach and wander.

---

## Adventure Seeds  
- A reflection tries to switch places.  
- The moon disappears from all mirrors.  
- A reflected version of the town appears below.

---

## Gateway to Gloamsorrow  
A moonlit pool that shows a darkened version of the diver before swallowing them.
