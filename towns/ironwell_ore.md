# Ironwell  
### Forge of the Deep • Hall of Makers

A town built around a meteorite core, Ironwell is equal parts forge, mine, and innovation hub.

---

## Architecture & Aesthetic  
- Iron-veined stone buildings  
- Steamworks and gear-run lifts  
- Bridges of hammered metal  
- Anvil bells that ring at dawn

---

## Culture  
**Hammer Hour** — all forges ring in unison.  
**The Making** — annual competition of innovation.  
**Stoneblood Trials** — rites of endurance and craftsmanship.

---

## Notable NPCs  
- **Grenn Stonehand**, forge-mother  
- **Tockwin**, gearwright child prodigy  
- **Myrn Coalzip**, soot-sprite diplomat

---

## The Severing’s Wound  
Metal starts dreaming of shapes—  
tools shift into other tools overnight.

## Adventure Seeds
