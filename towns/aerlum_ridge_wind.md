# Aerlum Ridge  
### The Skybridge Village • Nest of Wandering Songs

Aerlum Ridge sits atop airy plateaus connected by swaying silk bridges.

---

## Architecture & Aesthetic  
- Silk-suspended walkways  
- Open-air homes  
- Feathered rooftops  
- Windchime orchards

---

## Culture  
**Skywriting** — messages written with powders in air currents.  
**The Drift Festival** — people release song-kites.  
**Feather Vows** — symbolic promises tied to feathers.

---

## Notable NPCs  
- **Whistling Venn**, wind-caller bard  
- **Aira Plumefoot**, courier who never touches the ground  
- **Breezlet**, hyperactive wind sprite

---

## The Severing’s Wound  
Wind sometimes carries away thoughts—  
literally stealing words from minds.

---

## Adventure Seeds  
- A song returns carrying a warning.  
- A bridge flies away.  
- A storm steals everyone’s names.

---

## Gateway to Galehollow Ruin  
A wind-cave that exhales the names of visitors.
