# Stormeid, the Roaring Spine  
### Thunder-Caller • Breaker of Skies • Aetherborn Titan

Stormeid is the electric heart of chaos and courage.

---

## Appearance  
A towering figure of living lightning,  
bones visible like crackling stormlines.

---

## Domains  
- Lightning, storms, aether  
- Boldness, adrenaline  
- Sudden insight  
- Untamed skies

---

## Symbols  
- Spiral thunderbolt  
- Lightning coil  
- Aetherglass  

**Colors:** electric blue, white  
**Material:** Charged Energon  

---

## Offerings  
- Static-charged feathers  
- Lightning sketches on bark  
- Windribbons tied to stormspires

---

## Shrine (Stormrest)  
A cliffside altar where lightning  
always strikes the same point.

---

## Minor Spirits  
- **Wispadoon Zapwhirl**  
- **Flickerfen Nimbolt**  
- **Twillik Cracklecoat**

---

## Corrupted Aspect  
### *Threxon Coilshard*  
In **Skybreaker Maw**, storm becomes frenzy.

---

## Mythic Wound  
Stormeid cannot sit in silence.

---

## Role  
His champions are sky-breakers,  
thrill-takers,  
and sometimes prophets hit by inspiration like lightning.
