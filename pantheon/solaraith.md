# Solaraith the Golden  
### Dawnblade • Beacon of Truth • Radiant Warden

Solaraith is light as revelation—  
hope made visible.

---

## Appearance  
A radiant warrior,  
body of shifting sunlight,  
mane like solar flares.

---

## Domains  
- Light, truth, revelation  
- Courage, heroism  
- Righteous fire  
- Dawn rituals

---

## Symbols  
- Radiant circle  
- Sword of light  
- Dawnstone  

**Colors:** gold, white  
**Material:** Brighthoar  

---

## Offerings  
- Polished mirrors  
- Victory oaths  
- Sunwater collected at dawn

---

## Shrine (Sunspire)  
A golden tower aligned to catch sunrise and sunset,  
casting beams across the valley.

### *Sol’Karoth, Burning Verdict*

## Mythic Wound

and certainty with righteousness.

---

## Role  
His champions inspire and illuminate—  
but must beware arrogance.
